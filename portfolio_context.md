# Personal Headquarters Portfolio - Source of Truth

## Vision

This is not a traditional portfolio.

This is a Personal Headquarters that serves four purposes simultaneously:

1. Resume Amplifier
2. Professional Identity Hub
3. Public Proof of Work
4. Personal Operating System

The website should help:

* Recruiters evaluate me in 10-15 seconds
* Freelance clients trust me
* Other builders learn about me
* Future me track growth and achievements

The website should feel alive, modern, and continuously evolving.

---

# Core Philosophy

Most portfolios answer:

"Who am I?"

This website answers:

* What have I built?
* How do I think?
* What am I learning?
* What am I exploring?
* Why should someone trust me?
* How can someone work with me?
